# Privacy Policy

**App:** QC Divers  
**Package/App Name:** QC Divers / DumpsterScout  
**Effective Date:** May 22, 2026  
**Last Updated:** May 22, 2026  

QC Divers (“QC Divers,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is a field-scouting, recovery-planning, route-planning, reporting, and local recordkeeping app. This Privacy Policy explains how QC Divers handles information when you use the app.

QC Divers is designed around a **local-first and privacy-minded** approach. Core app data is stored on your device by default. Optional account and sync features may store supported data in cloud services if you choose to use them.

> This policy is intended to describe the app’s privacy practices clearly. It is not legal advice. You should review it for your specific release, jurisdiction, Play Store disclosures, and final implementation.

---

## 1. Summary

QC Divers may help you save and manage:

- Field locations
- Dumpster spots
- Tire spots
- Cleanup sites
- Salvage/resource locations
- Custom locations
- GPS coordinates
- Photos
- Reports and notes
- Routes and route stops
- Collections
- Field sessions
- Gear checklists
- Reminders
- Optional account and sync data
- Local backups and imports

By default, your records are stored locally on your device. If you enable optional account or sync features, supported records may be uploaded to our backend service so they can be synced or backed up.

We do **not** sell your personal data.

We do **not** use your private field records for advertising.

We do **not** provide public social networking, public chat, or public user-to-user sharing as the primary purpose of the app.

## 2. Information We May Collect or Store

The app may collect, access, store, or process the following types of information depending on which features you use.

### 2.1 Location Information

QC Divers may store location-related information that you manually enter or choose to capture, including:

- Latitude and longitude
- Address or place description
- Saved location name
- Saved location type
- Route stops
- Field-session visits
- Map-related location records

Location information is used to help you save, view, organize, revisit, and route to field locations.

QC Divers does **not** share your current precise physical location with other users.

### 2.2 Photos and Media

If you use photo features, QC Divers may access or store:

- Photos you take in the app
- Photos you choose from your device
- Photo metadata used by the app
- Photo links or storage references if optional sync is enabled

Photos are used to document saved locations, reports, field conditions, and related records.

Private photos are intended to remain private unless you explicitly use a feature that uploads or syncs them.

### 2.3 Reports, Notes, and User-Created Records

QC Divers may store user-created content such as:

- Field reports
- Notes
- Safety notes
- Hazard notes
- Access notes
- Tire counts
- Cleanup status
- Salvage/resource notes
- Tags
- Ratings or grades
- Favorites
- Collections
- Route notes
- Session notes
- Gear checklist entries

This information is used to provide the app’s core field-scouting, reporting, organization, backup, and sync functionality.

### 2.4 Account Information

If optional account features are enabled and you choose to sign in, QC Divers may process:

- Email address
- Authentication session information
- User profile or account identifier
- Sync ownership identifiers

Account information is used only for account access, optional sync, cloud data controls, and account deletion features.

### 2.5 Voice Reporting

If you use voice reporting, QC Divers may request microphone access to convert speech into a report or note.

Voice input is used only when you start a voice-reporting action. You can use manual text entry instead.

### 2.6 Notifications

If you create reminders, QC Divers may request notification permission to show reminder alerts.

Notifications may be used for:

- Revisit reminders
- Cleanup reminders
- Tire pickup reminders
- Field follow-up reminders
- Other reminders you create

### 2.7 Backup and Import Files

If you use backup/import/export features, QC Divers may create or read local backup files that contain app records such as locations, reports, routes, photos metadata, collections, reminders, sessions, and checklist data.

Backup files may contain sensitive location and note data. You are responsible for where you store, share, or upload exported backup files.

### 2.8 Diagnostics and Logs

QC Divers is designed to avoid logging sensitive personal information such as exact coordinates, private notes, access tokens, local photo file paths, or private remote photo URLs.

If crash reporting or diagnostics are added in the future, this Privacy Policy and the Play Store Data Safety disclosures should be updated before release.

## 3. How We Use Information

QC Divers uses information to provide app functionality, including:

- Saving and organizing field locations
- Displaying locations on a map
- Creating and editing reports
- Attaching photos to records
- Planning routes
- Tracking field sessions
- Managing collections
- Managing gear checklists
- Scheduling reminders
- Showing analytics inside the app
- Backing up and restoring local data
- Syncing supported records if optional sync is enabled
- Managing account access if optional account features are used
- Supporting account and cloud-data deletion

We do not use your private field records for targeted advertising.

---

## 4. Local-First Storage

QC Divers is built to work offline.

Many records are stored locally on your device using the app’s local database. Local records may remain on your device unless you delete them, clear local data, uninstall the app, or restore/overwrite data using backup/import features.

If you do not enable optional sync, your core app records are intended to remain local to your device.

---

## 5. Optional Cloud Sync

If you sign in and enable optional sync, supported app records may be uploaded to our backend service for syncing or backup.

Synced data may include:

- Saved locations
- Reports
- Photos or photo storage references
- Routes and route stops
- Collections and collection items
- Field sessions and visits
- Reminders
- Gear checklists
- Sync status and conflict metadata
- Account/profile identifiers

QC Divers uses sync status indicators and a sync queue to help show when records are pending, synced, failed, or in conflict.

If sync is disabled or you are signed out, supported records may remain local-only until you choose otherwise.

QC Divers may use Android system services for:

## 7. Sharing of Information

QC Divers does not sell personal data.

QC Divers may share or transmit data only in limited situations:

1. **With service providers** that support app functionality, such as optional sync, authentication, storage, or maps.
2. **When you choose to export, back up, share, or transfer data** using device or app features.
3. **For legal or safety reasons** if required by law, court order, valid legal process, or to protect rights, safety, or security.
4. **With your direction or consent**, such as if you choose to enable a future sharing-related feature.

QC Divers is not designed as a public social network or chat app.

## 9. Data Retention

Local app data may remain on your device until you:

- Delete individual records
- Clear local data in the app
- Import a backup that replaces data
- Uninstall the app
- Delete app storage through Android settings

If optional sync is enabled, synced data may remain in cloud storage until you delete it, delete your account/cloud data, or request deletion.

Some limited data may be retained if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or backup integrity, where applicable.

## 10. Data Deletion

QC Divers is intended to provide controls for:

- Deleting individual records
- Clearing local app data
- Deleting local photos or photo records
- Exporting data before deletion
- Deleting cloud data if optional sync is used
- Requesting account deletion if account features are enabled

If you created an account, you may request account deletion through the app where available or by contacting us.

Account deletion requests should delete or initiate deletion of the app account and associated cloud data, except where retention is legally required or otherwise permitted.

## 11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect user data.

These may include:

- Local-first storage
- Optional authenticated sync
- Private storage references for private photos
- Secure network transmission where cloud services are used
- Avoiding service-role secrets in the client app
- Avoiding sensitive information in logs
- Row-level security and storage policies where Supabase sync is configured

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your device, account credentials, backups, and exported files.
